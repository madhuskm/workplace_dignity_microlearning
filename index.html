Workplace Dignity Microlearning

Safe, Respectful & Inclusive Workplaces

This microlearning module helps Indian employees understand the essentials of Prevention of Sexual Harassment (POSH) as required under Indian law and company policy.
Mandatory for all employees. Estimated completion: 5 minutes.

Key Concepts

  • What is Sexual Harassment?
    Any unwelcome, sexually determined behaviour (verbal or physical) at the workplace that violates an individual's dignity.
  • Legal Framework
    The POSH Act, 2013, makes it mandatory for Indian companies to prevent, prohibit, and redress sexual harassment at work.
  • Examples
    Unwanted touching, inappropriate jokes, explicit messages, comments about appearance, repeated unwelcome advances.
  • Reporting
    All employees have the right to report an incident safely and confidentially to the Internal Committee (IC) or HR contact.
  • Employer Responsibility
    Every company must take prompt action, safeguard complainants from retaliation, and provide mandatory training to all employees.
